Terms & Conditions

Please read these terms carefully before placing an order with Bakenovation.

1 Payment Policy

All orders placed with Bakenovation require full payment in advance. We do not offer Cash on Delivery (COD) under any circumstances.

  • All payments made are strictly non-refundable once an order is confirmed.
  • Payment must be completed before your order enters production.
  • In the event of a cancellation initiated by Bakenovation, appropriate compensation or rescheduling will be arranged.

2 Order Policy

  • All orders must be placed at least 48 hours (2 days) in advance.
  • Custom cakes require additional lead time. Please discuss bespoke designs with our team before placing your order.
  • We reserve the right to decline orders that cannot be fulfilled within the requested timeframe.
  • Order confirmations are sent via WhatsApp or email after payment is received.

3 Delivery Policy

  • Delivery is available within our serviced areas. Please confirm delivery availability for your location before ordering.
  • Delivery time slots: Morning (10AM–1PM), Afternoon (1PM–4PM), Evening (4PM–7PM), Night (7PM–10PM).
  • This product is perishable. Delivery will be attempted only once. If the recipient is unavailable, the order may not be re-delivered.
  • Bakenovation is not responsible for delays caused by incorrect address details provided at the time of ordering.

4 Product Disclaimer

  • The delivered product may vary slightly from images shown on the website or AI Studio, as each cake is an artisanal, handcrafted creation.
  • Colors, textures, and decorations may differ slightly due to the handmade nature of our products.
  • Allergen information: Please notify us of any allergies before placing your order. Bakenovation uses common allergens including dairy, gluten, eggs, and nuts.

5 Cancellation & Changes

  • Orders cannot be cancelled once placed and confirmed.
  • Design changes may be accepted up to 36 hours before the scheduled delivery, subject to feasibility.
  • Date changes are subject to availability and must be requested at least 24 hours before the original delivery date.

6 Account & Privacy

  • By creating an account, you agree to provide accurate personal information.
  • Your data (name, email, phone) is used solely for order processing and communication. We do not sell your data to third parties.
  • OTP-based verification is used for account security. Do not share your OTP with anyone.

7 Governing Terms

By placing an order with Bakenovation, you agree to all of the above terms and conditions. Bakenovation reserves the right to update these terms at any time. The most recent version of these terms will always be available at this page.
